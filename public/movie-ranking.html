The Best Movies According to Carl

My top 3 movies of all time.

Raiders of the Lost Ark

The most perfect popcorn movie of all time. I saw this movie alone at the movie theater when I was 11 and I was never the same again.John Williams' music had a lot to do with it.

Field of Dreams

So easily could have been hokey. An incredible story of fathers and sons where baseball is a metaphor for communication and reciprocation of affection. Treads a delicate balance. How can any movie whose last line, "Hey, dad, you want to have a catch?" succeed in total and not seem like a corny line?

Tootsie

I never thought that a film about a guy who dresses like a woman would be my go-to feel good movie whenever I feel bad, but Larry Gelbart's screenplay and all the performances are perfection.
